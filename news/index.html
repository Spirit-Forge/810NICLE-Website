810NICLE Day 2023 Official Announcement & Patreon Launch

Posted by Vahkiti, October 8th, 2022

The team behind 810NICLE day is happy to announce that 810NICLE Day will officially be returning in August 2023 to celebrate the 20th anniversary of BIONICLE: Mask of Light, as well as 810's own 5th anniversary! We'll have plenty more to share very soon, so be sure to stay tuned throughout the coming months for more announcements, and the start of event registration for 2023's festivities! If you would prefer to get in touch with us directly ahead of open registration, you can also reach us through our Discord server, or by email at 810NICLEDay@MaskOfDestiny.com.

Additionally, we'd also like to announce the launch of our official 810NICLE Day Patreon! If you're interested in a look behind the curtain, special backer rewards, or just want to help out the team, be sure to head on over to Patreon.com/810NICLEDay to learn more and, help make 810NICLE Day even better!
